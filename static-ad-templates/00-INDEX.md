## How to Use

1. **Pick a template** based on your product type and campaign goal
2. **Fill in placeholders** (`[BRAND]`, `[PRODUCT]`, `[BENEFIT_1]`, etc.)
3. **Choose a copy variation** (A, B, or C) or remix them
4. **Use the image generation prompt** with brand-specific details
5. **Reference real examples** for tone and style guidance

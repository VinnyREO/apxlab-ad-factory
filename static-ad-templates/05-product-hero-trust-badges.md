# Template 05: Product Hero + Trust Badges + Urgency Offer

## Description
Classic direct-response format. Large product hero shot center stage, flanked by benefit callouts, trust badges below, and a time-sensitive offer bar at the bottom. Premium, high-converting layout.

## Visual Layout

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  [PROVOCATIVE HEADLINE]     │
│                    [SEAL]   │
│                    "#1      │
│                    [Auth]   │
│                    Rec'd"   │
├─────────────────────────────┤
│                             │
│  [Copy point 1]   [PRODUCT] │
│  [Copy point 2]   [HERO    │
│  [Copy point 3]    IMAGE]  │
│                             │
│  [Trust] [Trust] [Trust]    │
│  Badge   Badge   Badge     │
│                             │
├─────────────────────────────┤
│ 🔥 UPTO [X]% OFF — TODAY   │
│    ONLY → [CTA]            │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

### Zones
- **Headline Zone (12%):** Provocative headline + authority seal in top-right
- **Hero Zone (50%):** Large product bottle/jar hero shot (right side) with benefit copy (left side)
- **Trust Zone (15%):** Row of 3-4 trust badges (Non-GMO, Third-Party Tested, Vegan, Made in USA)
- **Offer Bar (13%):** Urgency-driven discount strip with fire emoji, percentage off, "TODAY ONLY"

## Copy Structure

### Headline Formula
> "Not All [PRODUCT_TYPE] Is Created Equal"
> "[PAIN_POINT]? It's Not What You Think."
> "Support Your [BODY PART]. [OUTCOME]."

### Benefit Copy Formula
> ✓ [Outcome-driven statement]
> ✓ [Differentiator vs. competitors]
> ✓ [Clinical/research backing]

### Placeholders
- `[PRODUCT_TYPE]` — Product category
- `[AUTHORITY]` — "#1 Urologist Recommended" etc.
- `[BENEFIT_1-3]` — Three key benefit statements
- `[TRUST_1-3]` — Trust badges (Non-GMO, Third-Party Tested, Vegan, GMP Certified)
- `[DISCOUNT]` — Percentage off
- `[URGENCY]` — "TODAY ONLY" / "LIMITED TIME" / "THIS WEEK"

## Best For
- Supplements (prostate, testosterone, weight loss, vitamins)
- Products with clinical backing or authority endorsements
- Direct-response campaigns with discount offers
- Products targeting older demographics (40+) who respond to authority + urgency
- High-volume scaling (this is the workhorse DTC format)

## Design Tips
- Dark backgrounds make the product "pop" and signal premium quality
- Authority seal should be prominent but not dominant — it's supporting evidence
- Trust badges address the #1 supplement objection: "Is this safe/legit?"
- The urgency bar is the conversion driver — make it feel time-sensitive
- Gold on navy = pharmaceutical luxury (men's health)
- Keep benefit statements under 10 words each

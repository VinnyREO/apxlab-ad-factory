# Template 07: Offer / Discount Banner

## Description
Bold, promotional layout centered on the deal. Product shot flanked by benefit callouts, with a prominent discount code, free shipping, or bundle offer. Works for retargeting, seasonal promotions, and BFCM campaigns.

### Zones
- **Promo Header (12%):** Star/sparkle emojis + promo headline
- **Product + Benefits (55%):** Product hero (right) with 3 benefit callouts (left). Discount code badge overlapping product.
- **CTA Zone (15%):** Button with offer-driven CTA
- **Guarantee Bar (8%):** Free shipping + guarantee icons

## Copy Structure

### Promo Headline Formula
> "⭐ Save [DISCOUNT]% for a limited time only! ⭐"
> "🔥 Buy [X], Get [X] Free"
> "[SEASONAL] Sale — [DISCOUNT]% OFF Everything"

### Placeholders
- `[DISCOUNT]` — Percentage off (15-50%)
- `[CODE]` — Promo code (LOVE, SPRING25, FIRST20)
- `[OFFER_TYPE]` — % off / BOGO / free shipping / free gift
- `[BENEFIT_1-3]` — Product benefits
- `[URGENCY]` — Limited time, ends Sunday, this week only

## Copy Variations

### Variation A: Percentage Off + Code
**Headline:** ⭐ Save [DISCOUNT]% for a limited time only! ⭐
**Body:** [BRAND] [PRODUCT] — trusted by [STAT]+ customers for [BENEFIT_1].

✓ [BENEFIT_1]
✓ [BENEFIT_2]
✓ [BENEFIT_3]

Use code [CODE] at checkout.
🚚 Free shipping on all orders.

[CTA: Get [DISCOUNT]% Off →]

### Variation B: BOGO / Bundle
**Headline:** 🔥 Buy 1, Get 1 Free
**Body:** Double up on [PRODUCT] and save. Whether it's for you and a friend, or stocking up for the months ahead — now's the time.

Each [PRODUCT] includes:
✓ [BENEFIT_1]

✓ [STAT]-day supply

[CTA: Claim Your Free [PRODUCT] →]

**Headline:** Get [DISCOUNT]% off when you subscribe today

## Best For
- Retargeting campaigns (people who already know the product)
- Seasonal/holiday promotions (BFCM, Valentine's, New Year)
- Subscription-first brands
- Products with strong repeat purchase potential
- Email list growth (discount for first order)

## Design Tips
- Discount should be visible within 0.5 seconds of seeing the ad
- Star/fire emojis in the headline boost urgency perception
- Promo codes should be short, memorable, and thematic (LOVE, SPRING, FIRST)
- Include free shipping — it reduces friction more than extra % off
- Money-back guarantee badge reduces perceived risk
- Don't over-design — the OFFER is the hero, not the layout

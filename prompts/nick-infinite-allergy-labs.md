## Brand Brief: Infinite Allergy Labs

**Brand:** Infinite Allergy Labs
**Product:** At-Home Food Sensitivity Test Kit
**Price:** $349
**What it does:** Tests 88 common foods for sensitivities from home. Simple finger-prick blood test, mail it back, get results in days.
**Target audience:** Adults 25-55 experiencing unexplained bloating, fatigue, skin issues, digestive problems. Many have been misdiagnosed with IBS or told "it's just stress." Skews female 60/40.
**Tone:** Empathetic, educational, empowering. Not clinical/cold. Think "finally someone understands."
**Platform:** Meta (Facebook + Instagram) — Reels and Feed
**CTA type:** "Learn More" (not Shop Now — $349 requires education before purchase)

## Competitor Intelligence (Use This to Inform Angles)

### What's winning in this niche right now:

**Top Hook Patterns (from competitor analysis, Feb 2026):**
1. **Curiosity/Shock:** "Do you know which of these 88 common foods could be making you sick? 🍕 Dairy 🌾 Wheat 🥚 Eggs 🥜 Nuts — The answer may shock you" (adapted from Viome's highest-performing hook)
2. **Misdiagnosis Story:** "I was told I had IBS for 8 years. Turns out I was eating 6 foods my body couldn't tolerate." (from Jennifer Hughes format — 30-day run = validated)
3. **Root Cause Reframe:** "The problem isn't your food. It's that nobody tested which foods are YOUR triggers." (from Mool Health)
4. **Quiz/Curiosity Entry:** "Take the 2-min food sensitivity quiz" (Gundry MD runs 98 ads with this format)
5. **Failed Alternatives:** "Why foods explode in your stomach 🧐" → "Bloating and fatigue dragging you down? Discover the root cause" (Supply Life — direct competitor, 104 active ads)

**What competitors do well:**
- Supply Life: 40-45 sec videos, curiosity hooks + emoji, PAS structure, "Learn More" CTA
- Viome: Shock hooks ("superfoods that are harmful"), heavy social proof ("500,000+ people"), $100 off offers
- Curex: "Build tolerance, not fear" — empowerment angle, longer educational videos (1:20)
- Gundry MD: Quiz funnels dominate (98 ads!), long VSLs paired with short hooks

**Video length sweet spot:** 40-45 seconds for food sensitivity ads. :15 for offer announcements. 1:00-1:30 for testimonial/education.

**What works in health test ads:**
- Pain Point → Root Cause → Simple Solution (most common winner)
- Shock/Curiosity Hook (highest engagement)
- Personal Misdiagnosis Story (longest running)
- "Learn More" CTA for $300+ products
- Heavy emoji usage: 😕 pain, 🧐 curiosity, ✅ benefits, ✨ transformation

### Key differentiators for Infinite Allergy Labs:
- Tests 88 foods (more than most competitors who test 20-30)
- At-home convenience (no doctor visit)
- Results in days (not weeks)
- Simple finger-prick (not a full blood draw)

# Scene Library — UGC Starter Frame Settings

10-15 unique scenes in rotation per brand. Replace when CTR drops.

---

## Core Scenes (Every Brand Needs These)

### 1. Kitchen Morning Routine ☀️
**Funnel:** Hook / Solution
**Setting:** Bright modern kitchen, morning sunlight through windows, white marble countertop
**Action:** Preparing supplement (mixing, pouring, blending)
**Props:** Glass blender, fresh fruits, wooden cutting board, coffee mug
**Lighting:** Warm golden morning light from window, soft fill from white surfaces
**Best for:** Greens, meal replacement, daily vitamins

### 2. Bathroom Selfie / Mirror Shot 🪞
**Funnel:** Hook / Proof
**Setting:** Clean modern bathroom, good lighting, mirror visible
**Action:** Selfie holding product, or placing product on vanity
**Props:** Skincare items, toothbrush, towel, mirror
**Lighting:** Bright even bathroom lighting, ring light effect
**Best for:** Beauty, collagen, gummies, skin supplements

### 3. Post-Workout 💪
**Funnel:** Solution / Proof
**Setting:** Gym, home gym corner, or just finished working out
**Action:** Drinking shake, holding shaker bottle, mixing supplement
**Props:** Gym equipment (blurred), towel, water bottle, yoga mat
**Lighting:** Overhead gym lighting or natural window light
**Best for:** Protein, pre-workout, performance, energy

### 4. Work Desk / Home Office 💻
**Funnel:** Problem / Solution
**Setting:** Modern home office, laptop visible, organized desk
**Action:** Taking supplement while working, supplement on desk beside laptop
**Props:** Laptop, coffee, notebook, plants
**Lighting:** Natural window light, warm desk lamp
**Best for:** Nootropics, focus, stress relief, energy

### 5. Unboxing / Haul 📦
**Funnel:** Hook
**Setting:** Living room couch, bed, or kitchen counter
**Action:** Opening package, revealing product, genuine excited reaction
**Props:** Shipping box, packaging materials, multiple products
**Lighting:** Natural indoor light
**Best for:** All categories — universal hook format

### 6. On-the-Go / Car 🚗
**Funnel:** Hook (pattern interrupt)
**Setting:** Car interior, driver's seat or passenger
**Action:** Holding product, talking to camera, quick selfie
**Props:** Car interior, steering wheel blurred, sunglasses
**Lighting:** Natural daylight through car windows, some shadow play
**Best for:** Convenience positioning, busy lifestyle

## Secondary Scenes (Rotate Based on Brand)

### 7. Outdoor / Park 🌳
**Funnel:** Solution / Lifestyle
**Setting:** Park, hiking trail, backyard, beach
**Action:** Post-run supplement, picnic with product, walking with shaker
**Props:** Running shoes, backpack, picnic blanket
**Best for:** Active lifestyle brands, greens, hydration

### 8. Grocery Store / Shopping 🛒
**Funnel:** Comparison / Social Proof
**Setting:** Store aisle, checkout, walking out with bag
**Action:** Showing product vs alternatives, "I found this"
**Props:** Shopping cart, store shelves, bags
**Best for:** "Why I switched" narrative, comparison ads

### 9. Night Routine / Bedside 🌙
**Funnel:** Solution
**Setting:** Bedroom, bedside table, cozy lighting
**Action:** Taking supplement before bed, placing on nightstand
**Props:** Book, water glass, warm blankets, dim lighting
**Lighting:** Warm, low, cozy — lamp light
**Best for:** Sleep, stress, magnesium, relaxation

### 10. Meal Prep / Cooking 🍳
**Funnel:** Solution / Educational
**Setting:** Kitchen, actively cooking
**Action:** Adding supplement to recipe, smoothie bowl prep
**Props:** Cutting board, vegetables, bowls, blender
**Best for:** Meal replacement, greens, protein powder

## Specialty Scenes (Use for Specific Campaigns)

### 11. Before/After Split

## Scene × Platform Optimization

| Platform | Best Duration | Top Scenes | Notes |
|----------|--------------|------------|-------|
| TikTok | 9-15s | Car, POV, Unboxing | Raw, fast, pattern interrupt |
| Meta Reels | 15-30s | Kitchen, Bathroom, Workout | Slightly more polished |
| Facebook Feed | 30-60s | Testimonial, Meal Prep, Before/After | Can be longer form |
| YouTube Shorts | 15-30s | Educational, Comparison | Value-first hooks |

## Scene Rotation Rules
- Minimum 10 unique scenes per brand at any time
- Track CTR per scene — replace bottom performers monthly
- Never repeat same scene + same avatar more than 3x/month
- Seasonal rotation: add holiday, weather-appropriate scenes

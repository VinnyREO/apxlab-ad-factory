# Hook Formulas — First 3 Seconds

## Why Hooks Matter
- 73% of video ads fail in the first 3 seconds
- Users spend 1.7 seconds on mobile feed content
- Good hook rate benchmark: 30-45% (3-second views / impressions)
- A 40% hook rate = double the people see your offer vs 20%

## Hook Rate Benchmarks

| Rating | Hook Rate | Hold Rate (15s/3s) |
|--------|-----------|-------------------|
| Below Average | <20% | <30% |
| Average | 20-30% | 30-45% |
| Good | 30-40% | 45-60% |
| Excellent | >40% | >60% |

---

## The 7 Highest-Performing Hook Formulas

### 1. The Industry Secret (47% hook rate avg)
"Here's what [AUTHORITY FIGURES] use but never tell you."
- "The exact stack dermatologists take themselves but never recommend to patients"
- "The supplement your trainer actually uses — not the one they sell you"

### 2. The Algorithmic Call-Out (41% hook rate avg)
Reference the algorithm delivering this content to them.
- "If this showed up on your feed, you already know your gut health needs help"
- "The algorithm showed you this for a reason — your sleep is about to change"

### 3. The Skeptic Conversion Arc (39% hook rate avg)
"I thought [PRODUCT/CLAIM] was [SKEPTICAL TAKE]. I was wrong."
- "I thought ashwagandha was another TikTok wellness scam. I was dead wrong."
- "Everyone said this supplement was overhyped. Let me show you my bloodwork."

### 4. The Platform Native Reply (38% hook rate avg)
Display a skeptical comment, then address it directly.
- [Comment overlay: "This looks fake lol"] → "Okay, I keep getting this. Let me show you the raw results."

### 5. The POV Pain Point (35% hook rate avg)
"POV: You've tried [FAILED SOLUTIONS] and [PROBLEM PERSISTS]."
- "POV: You've spent $500 on sleep supplements and still can't stay asleep past 3am"
- "POV: It's 2pm and you're on your third coffee but still can't focus"

### 6. The Reverse Psychology Disqualifier (34% hook rate avg)
"Don't buy [product] if you [actually want the benefit]."
- "Don't buy this magnesium if you hate sleeping through the night"
- "3 reasons NOT to try this protein powder" (ironic positives)

### 7. The Curiosity Gap Listicle (31% hook rate avg)
"Here are [NUMBER] reasons why I [DRAMATIC ACTION] for [PRODUCT]."
- "3 reasons I fired my nutritionist for this $40 supplement stack"
- "5 things nobody tells you about collagen. Number 4 changed everything."

## Additional Proven Hook Templates

### Social Proof Hooks
- "I finally got my hands on this viral TikTok supplement"
- "My friend's mom, who's a nutritionist, couldn't stop raving about this"
- "This product was on the news three times last month"

### Problem-First Hooks
- "If you haven't tried [X]... watch this"
- "I tried every [category] out there — here are the results"
- "Stop doing [wrong thing] and switch to this instead"

### Curiosity Hooks
- "Things TikTok made me buy — supplement edition"
- "Getting my daily [nutrient] hits different with..."
- "Want [specific outcome]? Stop scrolling"

---

## Three Types of Hooks (Layer These)

1. **Auditory:** Sound effects (claps, snaps), trending sounds, tonal voice shifts
2. **Visual:** .5 camera view, transitions, green screen, unexpected visuals
3. **Word:** Bold opening statement, thought-provoking question, counter-intuitive claim

**Critical:** Verbal hook and visual hook must work together = "compound attention lock"

## Platform Notes
- **TikTok/Snapchat:** Sound-on — audio hooks critical
- **Facebook/Instagram:** 80%+ watch muted — captions mandatory
- **Always** include captions/text overlays regardless of platform

# Brand Brief: Infinite Allergy Labs / Infinite Wellness Labs

## Company Overview
- **CEO:** Nick Jooma
- **HQ:** Duluth, GA
- **Type:** CLIA-certified clinical laboratory specializing in food allergy & sensitivity testing
- **Size:** 11-50 employees
- **Certifications:** CLIA certified, COLA accredited

## Brand Identity
- **Tagline:** "We help you find the root cause"
- **Tone:** Empathetic, educational, empowering. Medical credibility with consumer-friendly delivery.
- **NOT:** Cold/clinical, salesy, hype-driven
- **Think:** "Finally someone understands what you're going through"

## Target Audiences
1. **Primary (DTC):** Health-conscious adults 25-55 with unexplained symptoms (bloating, fatigue, skin issues, digestive problems). Many misdiagnosed with IBS or told "it's just stress." Skews female 60/40.
2. **Secondary (B2B):** Healthcare providers, functional medicine doctors, nutritionists
3. **International:** Nutritionists in Middle East, South America

## Brand Voice Guidelines
- Use "supports," "helps identify," "may be causing" — NOT "cures," "fixes," "eliminates"
- Lead with empathy for the struggle, then empower with the solution
- Reference the science (CLIA-certified, 4 immune markers) without being clinical/dry
- Speak like a knowledgeable friend, not a doctor lecturing

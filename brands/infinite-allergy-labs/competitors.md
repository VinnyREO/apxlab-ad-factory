# Competitors: Infinite Allergy Labs

## Direct Competitors

### Everlywell (THE main competitor)
- **Price:** $159-$259 for food sensitivity test
- **Tests:** IgG only (1 immune marker vs Nick's 4)
- **Strengths:** Massive ad spend, retail presence (CVS/Target), influencer marketing, 6,974+ reviews, FSA/HSA accepted, free shipping
- **Weaknesses:** Only tests 1 immune marker, misses IgE/IgG4/complement reactions
- **Ad strategy:** Hundreds of UGC video ads, "tired of visiting three different specialists?" angle, new mom testimonials
- **Ad volume:** 12+ active Meta ads at any time
- **Key hook:** Convenience + comprehensive ("one test vs multiple specialists")

### Viome
- **Price:** $149-$499
- **Tests:** AI-powered gut microbiome focus
- **Strengths:** 163 active Meta ads, heavy social proof ("500,000+ people"), shock hooks
- **Weaknesses:** Different test methodology (RNA vs antibody), more expensive for full suite
- **Best hooks to study:** "Do you know which superfoods could be harmful?" (curiosity/shock)
- **Offer strategy:** $100 off codes, aggressive discounting

### Supply Life (Closest direct competitor)
- **Price:** Mid-range food sensitivity tests
- **Active ads:** 104 on Meta
- **Best hooks:** "Why foods explode in your stomach 🧐" (40-45 sec videos)
- **Framework:** PAS — pain point → root cause → test kit
- **CTA:** Learn More (not Shop Now)

### Curex Allergy
- **Price:** Subscription immunotherapy
- **Active ads:** 92 on Meta
- **Angle:** "Build Tolerance, Not Fear" — long-term treatment, not just testing
- **Video lengths:** :18 to 1:30 (longer educational format)

### 5Strands
- **Price:** $88-$188
- **Tests:** Hair analysis (less clinical, more consumer)
- **Strengths:** Lower price point, Amazon presence
- **Weaknesses:** Not CLIA-certified, different methodology

## Competitive Advantages for Nick
1. **4 immune markers vs 1** — "We catch reactions that Everlywell misses"
2. **CLIA-certified** — hospital-grade lab, not just a consumer kit
3. **88 foods** — more comprehensive panel
4. **Supplement upsell** — post-test recurring revenue (competitors don't have this)
5. **Insurance accepted** — can be cheaper than Everlywell for insured patients
6. **Doctor-backed** — provider network adds credibility

# Compliance Reference — Supplement Ad Rules

## ❌ PROHIBITED (Ad Rejection / Account Ban)

### Disease/Cure Claims
- ❌ "Cures arthritis" / "Prevents colds" / "Treats insomnia"
- ❌ "Clinically proven to cure..."

### Guaranteed/Specific Results
- ❌ "Lose 10 pounds in a week"
- ❌ "Guaranteed to reduce joint pain in 30 days"
- ❌ "Works in 24 hours" / "100% effective"

### Negative Self-Perception
- ❌ Before/after implying "perfect" body type
- ❌ Shaming language about appearance/health
- ❌ "Are you tired of looking/feeling [negative]?"

### Prohibited Words
- ❌ "Cure" / "Treat" / "Heal" / "Fix"
- ❌ "Guaranteed results" / "Instant relief"
- ❌ "Clinically proven" (without extraordinary evidence)
- ❌ "Diagnose" / "Symptoms" / "Conditions" / "Diseases"
- ❌ "Most effective" / "Strongest formula"

### Body Function Claims Without Substantiation
- ❌ "Boosts metabolism" / "Cleanses toxins" / "Balances hormones"
- ❌ "Improves circulation" / "Burns fat"

---

## ✅ COMPLIANT (Safe Approaches)

### Structure/Function Claims (FDA-allowed)
- ✅ "Supports healthy sleep patterns"
- ✅ "Promotes balanced energy throughout the day"
- ✅ "Helps maintain joint comfort"
- ✅ "Supports your body's natural immune response"

### Ingredient-Focused
- ✅ "Contains ashwagandha, which clinical studies suggest may help manage stress"
- ✅ "Made with turmeric, traditionally used to support joint comfort"
- ✅ "Formulated with clinically evaluated ingredients"

### Experience-Based Language
- ✅ "Customer surveys report many users noticed improved comfort"
- ✅ "Perfect for those looking to enhance their nighttime routine"
- ✅ "Designed to support your wellness journey"

### Third-Party Credibility
- ✅ "NSF-certified for purity and potency"
- ✅ "Third-party tested for quality"
- ✅ "GMP-certified facility"

### Generalized Feedback
- ✅ "Customer surveys show high levels of satisfaction"
- ✅ "Over [X] 5-star reviews"
- ✅ "Featured in [legitimate publication]"

---

## High-Performing Compliant CTAs
- "Get yours today — link in bio"
- "Try it risk-free with their [X]-day money-back guarantee"
- "Grab yours before they sell out AGAIN"
- "Get [X]% off today with code [CODE]"
- "Start your wellness journey — link below"

## FTC Rules for UGC
- Paid partnerships must disclose: "Paid partnership with [Brand]" at start of video
- Don't rely solely on Meta's "Paid Partnership" tool
- Consumers must know the brand-creator relationship before engaging
